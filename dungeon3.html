The Dungeon


You arrive at the dungeon.

As you approach following the directions given, you see a small stone structure within a small clearing surrounded by trees. Its entrance surrounded by stone covered in plant life which seems to decay further down the structure. As you enter the structure you see a man sitting plainly on the floor, motionless until you arrive. He bids you a welcome and an abrupt farewell. It is Gilzaq, and he does not wish for you to be here. He meets your eyes and waits for your action.

Mention his tragedy? Resolve forcefully?
