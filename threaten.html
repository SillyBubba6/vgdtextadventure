The Threat


You threaten the people.

Your aggravated tongue is not appreciated, they take much offense and wish to see you disposed of. The leader has a look of pity on their face, for they know your fate. The town organizes a small execution including a makeshift gallows they put together for the event. As you stand on the platform staring death in the eyes, you reflect and realize it could have gone much better. On the bright side, you will be a local tale for many years to come, none showing you as the hero.
