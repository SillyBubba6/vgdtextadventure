The Artifact


You retrieve the artifact.

You walk towards the broken man while unsheathing your sword. Your footsteps echo the hall with each stride, bouncing off of the enclosed stone walls. You ready your sword for a piercing blow. Your sword pierces his skin and lunges deeper until the blade extrudes from his back, your blade covered the viscous red liquid. You grab the artifact from his satchel and Gilzaq grabs your shoulder. "Thank you, friend, I can see her. . ." He musters to say as the life fades from his eyes, leaving the corpse of a man who lived a tragedy.
