The Woods


You arrive in the woods.

You can see that the paths definite form starts to fade as you get further away from the peaceful aura of the thorp. Continuing using your best judgement you come to an intersection between environments. On the left a barren plains with slight hills distributed evenly, there seems to be small structures bunched together seemingly abandoned. On the right a lush forest progressively getting darker as you view into its intertwining branches with a damp and hazy feeling in the air.

Go to the open plains? Go to the dark trees?
