The Dungeon


You follow the directions.

You listen to the directions given to you and you soon arrive to a small clearing surrounded by trees, with a small stone structure in the middle. The structure being your destination you go inside its deep innards delving into the stone cool to the touch. As you reach the bottom you see a man sitting calmly on the floor, he acknowledges your presence with a welcome, followed quickly with an unexpected goodbye. You acknowledge him as Gilzaq. Your appearance here is not wanted, Gilzaq gazes at you to see your next move.

Resolve forcefully? Resolve diplomatically?
