The Tavern


You enter the tavern.

As you walk into the tavern you smell the faint tanginess of booze in the air, massive crowds have gathered speaking drunken cries of cheer and laughter. Many welcome you in and wish to have a drink with a bartender ready at your command. Amongst all of the chaos and persuasive drunkards there is a single man sitting with a half drank glass. As you approach he notices your attire and approves of your trade, clearly a man of similar work. He offers to give information if wanted amongst the cluster that is the tavern.

Grab a drink? Ask about Gilzaq?
