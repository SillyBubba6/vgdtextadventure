The Explanation


You explain to the people.

As you explain your intrigue of the seemingly abandoned houses the townspeople's nerves seem to settle. Those shouting accusations apologize promptly and all return to their respective duties. The leader cuts you down from the pole and asks of your reasoning out here. As you explain your task of the artifact he seemingly understands and is willing to direct you to the dungeon where Gilzaq is located. He warns that Gilzaq may be mistreated but leaves that judgement to you.

Ask for directions? Ask about Gilzaq?
