The Town


You arrive at the town.

You approach the town and see the town bustling with activity for how little people live there. You can see the market with many salesmen persuading their products to passerbys. As you reach the center of the town you see a small tavern with a stream of people flowing into it for time to relax. Many citizens are in the residential district walking about and spreading town gossip and news.

Go into the tavern? Find help?
