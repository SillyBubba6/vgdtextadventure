The Dungeon


You arrive at a dungeon.

As you push onward through the terrain a clearing approaches with a dark decrepit looking stone building leading downward near the middle. You proceed downwards into the depths of the stone and come across a man sitting in the middle of the tomb like bricks creating the surroundings. He turns to you and bids you a welcome following shortly with a stern goodbye. He makes it clear your presence is not appreciated, you recognize him as none other than Gilzaq, your target. Gilzaq turns to meet your gaze, he knows why you are here.

Resolve diplomatically? Resolve forcefully?
