The Start


You find yourself standing outside of a small thorp filled with hope and prosperity, the smell of fresh baked goods fills the distant air as you see villagers commencing commerce joyously. Knowing your target of Gilzaq, a supposed thief of a local artifact, you are to dispose of them by any means necessary. Jobs like these are not meant to be clean. You see a path to the local woods to the side of the thorp that clearly has been used repeatedly with its trodden on grass.

Go to the town? Go to the woods?
